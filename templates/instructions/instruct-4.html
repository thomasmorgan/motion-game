Instructions

In total you will complete trials, each of which involves reproducing a different dot motion. In all cases the dot motions are 5 seconds long and when giving your response the dot will disappear after 5 seconds. The experiment will take around 10 minutes and you will be paid $1 for taking part. You can also earn a bonus of up to $1 depending on how accurate your responses are.
