Instructions

In this experiment you will move a dot around a canvas using your cursor. You will be given information about the correct motion of the dot and you will need to match it as closely as possible.

The more accurate you are the bigger your bonus will be.

Your accuracy will be scored both in terms of the path of the dot, but also its speed. So even if you get the path totally right, if you move the dot too quickly or too slowly your bonus will be reduced.
