Instructions

In this experiment you will reproduce the motion of a dot by moving your cursor around a canvas.

Your accuracy is measured in terms of how accurately you reproduce the path and speed of the dot. The more accurate you are the bigger your bonus will be.

On each trial you will first watch the dot moving. However, you won't see all of the movement, only a section of it. For instance, you might see the final 2 seconds. In addition, you will see a stuttery replay of the response submitted by a previous participant.
