Instructions

To make the task harder you will not be given perfect information about the motion you will reproduce. Instead you will receive two types of information:

First you will see a section of the true motion of the dot. For instance, you might see the first 2 seconds of the motion.

Then you will be shown the motion submitted by a previous participant, but with noise that makes the dot "stutter".

Previous participants have also seen a section of the true motion (which many not be the same section you saw) as well as the "stuttery" submission of the participant before them.


The canvas below shows you what you will see, use the buttons to see the true motion of the dot, as well as a partial section of the dot and a stuttery version.
