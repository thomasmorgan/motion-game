Instructions

To make the task harder you will not be given perfect information about the motion you will reproduce. Instead you will receive two types of information: First you will see a section of the true motion of the dot. For instance, you might see the final 2 seconds of the motion. Then you will be shown a stuttery replay of the motion submitted by a previous participant.

The canvas below shows examples of what you will see. Use the buttons to see the true motion of the dot, as well as a partial section of the dot's motion and a version where the dot stutters. Note that with the partial section you might not get to see the starting point of the dot, but you should try to reproduce the motion from its start point, not just the start of what you saw.
