Let's do a practice trial. The canvas below is where you will move the dot, its border will go green when ready. The Section button plays part of the dot's movement, the Stutter button plays a stuttery replay of the previous participant's response, the Replay button plays back your most recent input and the Submit button submits your response.

You can click the Section, Stutter and Replay buttons and re-draw the dot's movement as many times as you wish. Only when you click Submit is the trial complete and only your final input affects your bonus.
